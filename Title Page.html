Arirang, the traditional sound of Korea



Arirang, also considered as an unofficial national anthem of Korea, is a song known to
all Korean people and regarded as their cultural identity. Arirang is the most famous Korean
traditional folk song that is listed in the UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage.
