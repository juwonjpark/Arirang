Arirang has been registered on UNESCO’s Intangible Cultural Heritage of Humanity list in 2012. The U.S. Army's 7th Infantry Division adopted "Arirang" as its official march song in May 1956. Arirang was played in PyeongChang 2018 Winter Olympics, especially during the Opening Ceremony and 2018 Asian Games as the Korean Unification Team won gold medal on canoeing. Many people try to use the word of Arirang as the representative of Korean people. For example, Arirang TV and Arirang Radio are international English-language media stations run by the Korea International Broadcasting Foundation. These media try to announce Korean culture globally.
