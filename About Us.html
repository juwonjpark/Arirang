My name is Joseph Park, and I am the creator and designer of this website. For this design unit, I am creating a website that will help the development and improvement in personal and cultural expression. I have chosen to introduce Arirang (One of the most famous Korean traditional folk songs) to the audience. These days, young people prefer to listen to Pop songs rather than Korean traditional song. This has made the Korean folk songs to be forgotten slowly as the time goes. For this reason, my goal for creating this website is to make the audience be aware of this situation.
